SourceMX Academy Website

SourceMX Academy is the most complex project I have worked on and the project I have learned the most from. SourceMX Academy is an online digital skills academy where users can purchase a course and continue to follow it online.

Project Purpose and Goals

The project aimed to make the website simple so that the user could easily and effortlessly scan the crucial information about the courses and on the other hand, the page to have all the necessary pages such as blog posts, an events page, a contact page and longer academies. Another important goal was for each contact form to be linked to Zapier and send automated messages for successful course registration.

Web Stack and Explanation

For this project, we decided to use WordPress CMS for easier management of the site, i.e. to make adding new courses lighter. Another reason why the team decided to use WordPress was that not all team members were developers and were required to manage the site, such as posting their blog post on the site.

Problems and Thought Process

The biggest problem we faced was the translation. The topic we used was in English, and for the company's needs, the website needed to be in Macedonian. For that purpose, after some research, we decided to use the Loco Translate plugin that fully satisfied our needs. Because we did research and found that for longer courses, the landing pages perform and convert better, we decided to create the long courses as separate landing pages. To make the page look modern and beautiful, we used the Elementor PRO plugin.

Lessons Learned

With this project, I may be most proud of myself because, through the changes and the testing of various plugins and methods, I learned a lot and transferred all that knowledge to other projects: from the installation and setting of the theme to the complete finalization of the project. I had the opportunity to learn more about the importance of responsive design, user experience and user interface in one single project. To get acquainted with WooCommerce working, SEO, website speed, image optimization, installing Facebook pixel and Google Analytics and working with cPanel.
